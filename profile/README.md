## About Dead by Daylight

Dead by Daylight is a multiplayer survival horror game where players take on the role of either a killer or one of four survivors. Survivors must work together to repair generators, avoid the killer, and escape the map, while the killer hunts them down.

Each match offers a unique experience with different maps, characters, and abilities. The game emphasizes teamwork, stealth, and strategy, creating intense and unpredictable gameplay scenarios.
